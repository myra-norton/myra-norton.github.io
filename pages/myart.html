how i made this flipbook

1. pick a video and crop it
2. use ffmpeg to break video into frames
3. select frames (this one has 19)
4. remove image backgrounds in photoshop
5. put edited images through drawingbotv3
6. use pen plotter to draw each frame
7. scan each index card
8. use ffmpeg to out images back into a video at preferred frame rate (i used 7 fps)

how i made this animation

part 1: build a jig
1. use table saw to cut wood to desired size
2. use drill press to make a hole in each corner
3. use band saw to cut a rod into little pegs
4. sand pegs and insert them into the holes

part 2: make animation 1. pick a video and crop it
2. use ffmpeg to break video into frames
3. select frames
4. strategically place masks in adobe after effects (thank you tyler)
5. use cricut to cut out corner holes, subject, and sky
6. take photos and compile into video using snapdragon

how i made this piece

1. cut up some masking tape and place randomly
2. write some code in p5js for drawing concentric circles
3. use pen plotter with paint
4. fill in some spots by hand
5. trace edges with white pen

about this piece...

i had this image come to mind as i was falling asleep, so i got outta bed to sketch it real quick. i spent all of the next two days ripping up magazine pages, grouping the colors, cutting out the different shapes, gluing the pieces onto the shapes, and gluing the shapes into one cohesive piece.
